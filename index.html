Privacy Policy for FashionShop Inhambane

Effective Date: July 11, 2026

This privacy policy explains how FashionShop Inhambane collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Because our application does not process payments or share data with third parties, your information is kept private and used solely to facilitate your shopping experience.

1. Device Permissions We Request

To provide a functional shopping experience, the app requires the following core permissions:

  1. Internet Access: Used to load product catalogs and sync account details.
  2. Storage Access: Used to cache product images for faster browsing.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum amount of personal information necessary:

  1. Account Information: Names and email addresses for account management.
  2. Communication Information: WhatsApp phone numbers to contact you about orders.

3. Data Storage and Infrastructure (Firebase)

Your user data is securely stored using Google Firebase Cloud services.

  1. Data Purpose: We store this data to save your user profile, manage app preferences, track your order cart status, and seamlessly sync your information across your devices.
  2. Data Security & Encryption: Firebase secures your information by encrypting data in transit using industry-standard HTTPS/SSL protocols and encrypting data at rest on Google's cloud servers.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes only:

  1. To create and manage your user account.
  2. To communicate with you regarding your orders via WhatsApp.
  3. To improve the service and product offerings of our app.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your privacy is our priority. We never share, sell, rent, or trade your personal data (including your email address or WhatsApp phone number) with any third-party companies or advertisers. Firebase acts strictly as our cloud backend provider and does not use your data for its own purposes.

6. Your Data Rights

You have the right to review, update, or delete your personal information at any time. You can request to access or erase your data by contacting us directly via the in-app WhatsApp support feature.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us via the in-app WhatsApp contact system or at the developer contact provided in your app store submission.
